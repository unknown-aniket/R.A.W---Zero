The Problem We're Solving

Why Campus Life
Is Broken

📋

Attendance Chaos

Students have zero real-time visibility into their attendance %. By the time they find out, it's too late to fix it — detentions, exam blocks, parental calls.

📁

Study Material Scattered

PPTs on WhatsApp groups, notes in Google Drive, assignments via email. There's no single source of truth per subject, per class, per branch.

📅

Timetable Confusion

Handwritten timetables, last-minute class changes, no push notifications. Students miss lectures. Teachers don't know who's attending.

📊

CGPA In The Dark

Students calculate CGPA manually every semester using calculators and Excel sheets. Errors are common. Stress is constant.

🏖️

Leave Tracking is Manual

Teachers maintain leave records on paper or in personal spreadsheets. No central system, no transparency, no accountability.

🏫

Admins Are Disconnected

Department admins have no efficient way to manage university structure, branches, batches, or push updates to all students and teachers at once.

Our Project Goal

One App.
All Roles.
Seamless.

R.A.W – Zero is our hackathon submission — a role-based Android app built in Flutter + Firebase. The goal: give every person on a college campus exactly what they need, nothing more, nothing less.

First launch: Select your university and branch. After that? Direct login to your personalized dashboard — no friction, no confusion.

Role-Based Access

Designed For
Everyone On Campus

Every user gets a tailored experience. The right data, the right tools, the right permissions — nothing more, nothing less.

🎓

Student

Complete academic dashboard. Everything a student needs to stay on top of their academic life.

  • Real-time Attendance Percentage
  • Subject-wise PPT & Study Material
  • Daily & Weekly Timetable
  • Class Alert Push Notifications
  • Semester-wise CGPA Tracker
  • Final SGPA Calculator
📚

Teacher

Manage your classes, resources, and schedule without the manual paperwork.

  • Remaining Leave Balance
  • Class-wise PPT Upload & Access
  • Class Alert Notifications
  • Upcoming Schedule View
  • Branch & Section Management
  • Student Visibility per Class
🏫

Admin

Full control over the college ecosystem — departments, branches, students, and faculty.

  • Manage University Structure
  • Add/Edit Branches & Departments
  • Manage Student Accounts
  • Manage Teacher Accounts
  • Push Announcements to All
  • Control Access & Permissions
